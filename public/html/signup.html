Welcome to Pop!

Having trouble deciding on a movie to watch with a friend? Never fear, Pop! is here! Pop! analyzes you and a friend's watch history as well as a genre you'd like to watch to find a list of movie suggestions in that genre you could watch together. These lists are also saved in your account if you'd ever like to look for more movies.
